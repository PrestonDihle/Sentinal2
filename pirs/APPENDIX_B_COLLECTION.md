# Appendix B: OSINT Collection Cycle Checklist

**Classification:** UNCLASSIFIED // OPEN SOURCE ONLY

Use this checklist to maintain sustained collection on the PIR set. All sources are free-to-access. Native-language sources (Arabic, Farsi, Hebrew, Turkish) break events 6-12 hours ahead of English translation; prioritize accordingly for time-sensitive collection.

---

## Daily Collection

- Reuters and AP wire scan -- Middle East section -- reuters.com and apnews.com/hub/middle-east
- CENTCOM press releases -- centcom.mil/MEDIA/PRESS-RELEASES -- if US operations are active
- UKMTO and Combined Maritime Forces -- ukmto.org and combinedmaritimeforces.com -- if maritime relevant
- GPSJam.org -- EW and jamming indicators; new blobs over Lebanon or the Gulf often precede kinetic activity
- NASA FIRMS -- firms.modaps.eosdis.nasa.gov/map -- new thermal signatures in Iraq, Syria, or Yemen
- Telegram scan via tgstat.com -- regional channel growth spikes and posting frequency anomalies
- BonBast.com -- Iran parallel FX rate; daily movement is a leading economic pressure indicator
- ADS-B Exchange -- globe.adsbexchange.com -- unusual military callsign clusters toward forward staging bases
- OFAC Recent Actions (home.treasury.gov) -- daily sanctions designations; Russian and Chinese entities designated for Iran transfers are PIR 4 indicators
- TASS and Chinese MFA -- tass.com and mfa.gov.cn/eng -- scan for Russia-Iran and China-Iran bilateral statement language signaling deepening alignment

---

## Weekly Collection

- ACLED update download -- acleddata.com -- CSV or dashboard for Iraq, Syria, Yemen, Lebanon
- Kpler and Vortexa blog -- Iran crude export estimate and dark tanker tracking updates
- EIA weekly oil inventory -- eia.gov -- Wednesday publication; price pressure on GCC and global markets
- Sentinel Hub EO Browser -- apps.sentinel-hub.com/eo-browser -- imagery check on key IAMG facilities, Iranian nuclear sites, and Hormuz approaches
- IOM Displacement Tracking Matrix -- dtm.iom.int -- displacement trend check for Lebanon, Syria, and Iraq
- Windward AI free reports -- windward.ai/resources -- dark vessel and sanctions-evasion analytics update
- INCD and CISA ICS advisories -- gov.il and cisa.gov/ics-advisories -- cyber threat to critical infrastructure
- Kpler and Vortexa Iran crude estimates -- dark tanker tracking and Chinese absorption volume as PIR 4.2 sanctions-evasion indicator
- ISIS Nuclear Iran (isis-online.org) -- nuclear site satellite imagery update; Fordow, Natanz, Parchin as PIR 1.8 indicators
- ICG Crisis Watch Lebanon, Iraq, Jordan -- crisisgroup.org/crisis-watch -- buffer state institutional fragility as PIR 1.9 indicators

---

## Monthly Collection

- OPEC Monthly Oil Market Report -- opec.org; production quota adherence and price context
- IAEA Board of Governors report -- iaea.org/topics/iran -- if published; enrichment status and inspector access
- FAO Food Price Index -- fao.org/worldfoodsituation/foodpricesindex/en -- food security early warning
- WFP Situation Reports -- wfp.org/publications/situation-reports -- Yemen, Syria, Gaza IPC classification updates
- ICG Crisis Watch -- crisisgroup.org/crisis-watch -- monthly escalation and improvement status by country
- CRS and RAND new publications scan -- crsreports.congress.gov and rand.org -- MENA theater background and policy analysis
- UN Comtrade Iran export data -- comtrade.un.org -- food and specialty commodity export trend check (note 30-90 day lag)
- SIPRI Arms Transfers Database -- armstransfers.sipri.org -- annual update (January); Russia-Iran technology transfer tracking as PIR 4.1 indicator
- SCO official website -- eng.sectsco.org -- summit and meeting outcomes; trilateral Russia-China-Iran statement language as PIR 4.3 indicators
- DNI Annual Threat Assessment -- dni.gov -- released February; unclassified IC assessment of Iranian nuclear weaponization status; PIR 1.8 baseline
